India **DPDP Rules notified 2025-11-13**, phased: DPB immediate; consent-managers by 2026-11-13; **substantive obligations only from 2027-05-13 (hard enforcement)**. Through 2026 = "soft enforcement" (guidance/warnings). No restricted-jurisdiction/data-localization list published as of mid-2026 — cross-border transfers broadly permissible. Penalties up to ₹250 crore but post-2027.

**By contrast, RBI is in force today:** 2018 payments-data-localization + 2023 IT/cloud Master Direction already require core banking/payment data to stay in India; audited annually. RBI FREE-AI framework forthcoming.

**Why:** "DPDP requires this today" is the most over-claimed assumption in Routeplane's strategy and destroys credibility with any CISO who knows it's soft-enforcement until 2027.

**How to apply:** Lead India GTM with **RBI/sectoral compliance (live trigger), not DPDP**. Frame DPDP 2027-05 as the demand wave Routeplane positions *ahead of* ("be compliant before the deadline forces a scramble"). Re-verify the localization-list status before any pitch — if MeitY notifies the SDF restricted-list, the trigger strengthens materially.
