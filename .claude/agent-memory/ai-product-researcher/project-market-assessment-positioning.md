Conclusion of `docs/product/market-assessment-2026-06.md` (PR RST-Holdings/docs#7):

- **Most probable outcome = small profitable India compliance niche ($0.5–3M ARR), not venture-scale** on current trajectory. P(fold)~35%, P(niche base)~45%, P(good)~15%, P(venture)~5%.
- **The defensible wedge is NOT "agentic security" broadly** — Routeplane would lose a build race to PAN/Portkey + 10 funded pure-plays (Lasso, Prompt Security, MintMCP, Prisma AIRS) on detection/runtime, where it sits at ○/◐. The corner no one can reach = **sovereign, RBI/DPDP-resident, self-hostable agentic governance** (counter-positioning).
- **Sovereign per-request residency routing is genuinely uncontested** (whole field reads ○). The agentic half is "designed, not built" — the most likely internal failure mode is it never ships under solo bandwidth.
- **Recommended 2-quarter bet:** ship thinnest control plane whose hero feature is the **DPDP/RBI sovereign-residency audit artifact** (cheapest test of "will a regulated buyer pay?"), NOT the MCP/agentic layer first.
- **Fold trigger:** if by 2027-Q1 <3 logos buy the gateway+residency bundle AND inbound pull is for agentic-security/MCP-governance → fold gateway to a feature, re-found as sovereign agentic-security control point (security budget > platform budget).

**Why:** This is the honest investor-grade frame the founder asked for; supersedes any rosier "category is wide open" read in feature-matrix.md.

**How to apply:** Anchor future strategy on the *intersection* (sovereign + agentic + India-resident), not on agentic security in general. Lead with the audit artifact. See [[project-pan-acquired-portkey]] and [[project-dpdp-enforcement-timing]].
